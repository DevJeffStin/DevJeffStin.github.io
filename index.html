Jefferson Stinson is a developer currently at the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Arkansas, with years of experience. He has worked on many projects including 3 award winning websites and other large scale web software projects while working at DNT Media, along with working on large web systems within the courts. His languages of choice would be PHP, HTML, JS, and MySQL but he also has experience in languages such as C#, C++, PERL, Python and Visual Basic. He is also profiecent in server maintnence with Windows and Linux systems, both on the hardware and the software side, having experience maintaining both.

Besides development, Jefferson is an avid PC gamer, LEGO enthusist, photographer, gun collector, and trail rider. He mainly plays a variety of games which include Kerbal Space Program, Counter-Strike 2, Escape From Tarkov, Fallout, and the ocasional Fortnite. Being a LEGO enthusist he mainly builds Star Wars, and Harry Potter sets but also likes to build the more model space and car ones as well. Going out trail riding with his dad is one of his favorite hobbies, Arkansas has many beautiful locations to go out riding and taking an old jeep through the trails is always nice.
